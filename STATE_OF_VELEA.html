1 · What Velea is

A Vedic-timing companion: the engine locates a person on their karmic path from computed sky-data (Swiss Ephemeris, Lahiri sidereal, the canon of K&F Vols. I–II + Appendix IV), and the LLM only voices what the engine found. The founding wound it answers: June 2026, AI astrology gaslit David because it was handed a pile of sky with no lens. Velea's law is the opposite — the time frame picks the one system (the Lens Router), data accuracy outranks everything, and the path is computed, not fixed ("destined" is banned from the copy).

"Perception is a moving target. Velea is the horizon."
