## Tajika aspects & orbs (Ch.14) — for a live-aspect layer
Tajika uses the 5 Western aspects (unlike Parashari whole-sign drishti): **conjunction 0°, sextile 60°, square 90°, trine 120°, opposition 180°** — each ± an orb.
- **Deepthamsa orb table** (a planet's orb of influence): Sun 15°, Moon 12°, Mercury 7°, Venus 7°, Mars 8°, Jupiter 9°, Saturn 9°. Two planets aspect when they fall within each other's orbs; beyond its orb a planet loses influence. An aspect/yoga materializes only when within orb.
- **Nature:** trines = *pratyaksha mitra* (very friendly); sextiles = friendly; oppositions = *gupta shatru*/inimical (openly); squares = inimical (secretly/inwardly). **Rahu & Ketu are not included in Tajika yogas.**
- **Ithasala** = an *applying* aspect (the faster planet approaching) → the event/union forms. **Easarapha** = a *separating* aspect → it pertains to the past.

## Prasna timing framework (Ch.13, p350–352)
> Horary timing — principles transferable to event-timing.
- **Sign modality → unit of time:** Movable/Cardinal = days · Common/Mutable = weeks/months · Fixed = months/years. (Movable signs = quick; Fixed = slow, greater resistance.)
- **House group × modality table** (of the lagnesha/significator):
  | Group | Movable | Common | Fixed |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | Kendra (1,4,7,10) | Days | Weeks | Months |
  | Panaphar (2,5,8,11) | Weeks | Months | Years |
  | Apoklima (3,6,9,12) | Months | Years | Extended delay |
- Moon in the **6th, 8th or 12th** → delays/obstacles. **Saturn's aspect** on Ascendant/lagnesha/significator → restriction/delay (up to a year). Moon's **Ithasala** with both lagnesha and significator → quick results. Movable signs = immediate (days); Fixed/Common = slower (months/years). The event realizes when the significator/lagnesha **transits the Ascendant, its own sign, or its exaltation.**

## Appendix II — Prasna time / distance / height + places (p362)
- **Time by planet:** Sun 6 months, Moon 48 minutes, Mercury 2 months, Venus 2 weeks, Mars 1 day, Jupiter 1 month, Saturn 1 year. **Distance:** Sun Med, Moon Short, Mercury Med, Venus Long, Mars Med, Jupiter Med, Saturn Long. **Height:** Sun Avg, Moon Short, Mercury Short, Venus Avg, Mars Short, Jupiter Tall, Saturn Tall, Rahu Tall, Ketu Tall.
- **Places by planet:** Sun = temples, worship, authority/government; Moon = watery places, where people are nurtured/cared for; Mercury = libraries, schools, trade, sports/recreation; Venus = beds/sleeping apartments, cinemas, restaurants, places that please; Mars = hot/fiery places, factories, kitchens, cremation; Jupiter = culture, learning, banks, pilgrimage; Saturn = dirty/unclean, slums, decay, graveyards.
- **Places by element:** Fire = hot places / where fire is used (kitchens, ovens, factories); Earth = near the ground, farmland, wooded/rural; Air = light/airy, balconies, terraces, high places; Water = near water — wells, pools, bathrooms, rivers, sea, coast.

## Prasna MC/IC note (p354)
The **Midheaven (MC)** = the externalized point of ego/self-consciousness; the **IC** = the most internalized, private area. (Consistent with Velea's Meridian: MC = the received/public self, IC = the private ground.)
