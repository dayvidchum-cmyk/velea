## CORE THEME
This is a year about what you **believe** — and the belief on trial is **what you're
worth.** Venus, your year's planet, rules both your **9th house** (belief, conviction,
what you live by) and your **2nd** (worth, money, value), and sits in your **5th** (love,
pleasure, your own expression). So worth, money, love, and pleasure are one question: do
you believe in your worth, or is it still **dependent on what others think of you?**
You've handed the verdict out — *proving* yourself to earn a good opinion, then accepting
**less than you're worth** when it comes. Take the verdict back. Your worth isn't earned
or granted — it's yours. Hold it as your own conviction.

## WHY NOW — the dasha
Your long-cycle lord, **Jupiter**, sits in your **2nd house** (money, self-worth, your
voice) and rules your **4th** (home, mother) and **7th** (partnership) — the years-long
push is to grow your worth, and it runs through your home and your relationships. Your
sub-period, **Ketu**, sits in your **4th house** — your roots: your mother and father,
yes, but more than them — *everything that planted what you believe about yourself.*
It's loosening the early *"not enough"* at its source. And **Saturn**, ruler of your 5th,
sits on your **rising sign** — the prove-it judge isn't out in the world, it's built into
your sense of self. This season is for letting that old verdict **dissolve**, not
rebuilding it. (Note the axis: your 4th — where the belief was planted — is the *source*
of your 9th — the belief you now live by. Uproot the first to change the second.)

## MANIFESTATIONS
- **Money & Love — one wound, two rooms.** Venus runs both, so they move together. Where
  you're underpaid, look at where you're under-loved; where you're proving for a raise,
  you're proving for affection. Name what you're actually worth — in salary and in how
  you're treated — and ask. Same boundary, two doors.
- **Pleasure.** Your year's planet sits in your house of romance and delight, in a sign
  that rations it. You've been deferring joy and earning love. Let pleasure and affection
  in **without proving you deserve them.**
- **Home & roots.** The grief surfaces at the foundation — your parents, yes, but more:
  *whatever shaped what you believe about yourself.* This is for releasing the source of
  the "not enough," not patching it back into the old shape.

## THE LESSON
Stop proving what's already yours, and stop accepting the discount. The long journey this
year is moving your worth **from others' opinion to your own conviction** — then
**voicing it.** When the pay, the love, or the respect lands under what you're worth, that
friction isn't a verdict on you; it's the signal showing you exactly where a **boundary**
has to hold.
