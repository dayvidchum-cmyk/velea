## The structure to mirror (David: "start with the dominant force, then spiral down and focus")

1. **The dominant force / the season** — open on the biggest thing shaping the month: the Time Lord
   (dasha) season AND its dated SUB-PERIODS (the moving parts), not just the standing lord.
2. **Background positive (entire window)** — the single strongest supportive standing transit, named as
   a deliberate-use gift ("Jupiter exalted in the 11th = strongest income/alliance transit in 12 years").
3. **Background pressure (entire window)** — the heaviest standing transit to pace against ("Saturn
   exact opposition to lagna — partnership weight, fatigue. Must pace.").
4. **Key dates** — the specific, dated beats, each with the concrete life-area + the do/don't.
5. **Net read** — a one-paragraph phase pattern that lands the month ("June build quietly. Late July
   defensive driving. Aug 13–22 launch window. Jupiter in the 11th rewards whatever you ship.").
