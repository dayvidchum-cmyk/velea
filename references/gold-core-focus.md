## The read

Not primarily a "travel and luck" year. It's: **building systems of knowledge
that serve, teach, heal, diagnose, or improve something.**

- **9th house brings:** teaching, philosophy, publishing, higher learning,
  guidance, frameworks, meaning.
- **Venus in the 6th channels all of it through:** work, service, daily practice,
  solving problems, refinement, helping people, improving systems.
- **Aquarius (Venus's sign) adds:** systems thinking, pattern recognition,
  technology, unconventional approaches, future-oriented ideas, frameworks that
  help many people.
- **Shatabhisha (Venus's nakshatra — "hundred healers") adds:** diagnosis,
  finding what is broken, uncovering hidden patterns, correction, truth-seeking,
  systems of healing/repair, seeing through illusion. "What is actually happening
  beneath the surface?"

**Developmental task:** Learn → Systematize → Teach. Not merely consume or
believe — build a framework that explains, organizes, diagnoses, and serves.

**One-sentence synthesis:** "A year of becoming a teacher, system-builder, and
diagnostician — using disciplined work and pattern recognition to transform
knowledge into something that can genuinely help other people."

1. **Weave the Time Lord's SIGN qualities.** The sign the year lord occupies
   (here Aquarius) is HOW the year operates — its style of mind and method. The
   engine underuses it. Every Core Theme should carry the occupied sign's mode.
2. **Weave the Time Lord's NAKSHATRA.** The nakshatra (here Shatabhisha) carries
   the deeper aim/symbol beneath the sign — often the sharpest, most specific
   signal. The engine barely touches it. Name its symbol/theme in the read.
3. **Fuse house + sign + nakshatra into ONE vocation/identity**, not separate
   bullet facts (already the RULERSHIP CHAINS intent — extend it to sign+nakshatra).
4. **Frame a developmental ARC** (e.g. Learn → Systematize → Teach) in the
   Developmental Task, not a static description.
5. **Name what the year is NOT** ("not a travel-and-luck year") to correct the
   generic textbook reading of the house.
